CH-Payments

Welcome to CH Payments, a simple paymentprovider wrapper for stuff you want CH to get money for. Payments makes it easy to create products, and implement it in your own website. No hassle with keys, accounts and webhooks, just a single call to initiate a payment.

Below you can find some ready-to-use code examples for your website.

The administration panel (for now) is only available for board members. They can create products to generate the keys required.
